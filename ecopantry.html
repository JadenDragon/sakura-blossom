EcoPantry

About the Project

    EcoPantry was a group project we worked on within my 2nd year of CMGT at Saxion. The goal of the project was to create our own start-up company and decide on a core product in the form of an app or game. We settled on EcoPantry, a prototype pantry app for users to keep track of their food & expiration dates at home and can also generate recipes with the ones that you already own.

    With the launch of EcoPantry, we started a campaign called the #PantryChallenge that would promote the app. It was started as a challenge for users to share their unique and bizarre recipe combinations. This was a feature that EcoPantry has to generate recipes from bizarre ingriedient combinations.

My contributions

    In this project I was given the role of scrum master to divide the workload within our group and ensure the completion of our product within the timeframe. Alongside this, I was responsible as the user experience designer of the app and for performing user test & iterations of the app design.
