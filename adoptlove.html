Adopt Love

About the Project

    Adopt Love is a group project that we worked on to create an interactive, 3D game that matches the goal of an existing advertising campaign that raises and/or creates awareness for public and/or social issues. Our goal was to raise awareness on animal shelters, that way we would inspire more people to adopt a pet, so that it would lower the burdens of animal shelters.

    Adopt Love is a game about pet adoption where the player needs to donate as many pets as possible to a loving home. The game takes place inside a pet shelter in a suburban town. The game is about managing your resources while also donating as many pets as possible, while matching them with an owner that fits ther characteristics and needs.

My contributions

    In this project I was given the role of scrum master to divide the workload within our group and ensure the completion of our game within the timeframe. I was assigned the role of UI/UX designer for our team and worked collaboratively with our UI artist to design fun & cohesive interfaces for our game, which would allow a more seemless user experience. I was also given the responsibility for performing user tests of the gameplay and overall user experience, so that we could improve on these wherever necessary.
